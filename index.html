TRAINING SERVICES

ONLINE PERSONAL TRAINING

We write your program based upon your available equipment, the amount of times per week you desire to workout, the available time you have, your injuries or limitations, past history and current status and goals which may include nutrition and weight management. You will receive a weekly schedule, mapping out each day and the exercises prescribed for your program, including strength and conditioning workouts and recovery days. Great for those who desire guidance and those just getting started.

1 ON 1 PERSONAL TRAINING

Our certified BootyBodyYouth personal trainers get to know you, your goals and concerns before developing a personalized training program to help you achieve your health and fitness goals. Our personal trainers keep you motivated by providing ongoing feedback, tips, nutrition guidance workout tracking & progress along with frequent adjustment of your training program if needed.

VIRTUAL PERSONAL TRAINING

We come to you on your laptop, mobile device or desktop and work with you to get in shape from wherever you are. Virtual personal training fully recreates the face to face experience with dedicated one-on-one instruction for a full hour on your preferred platform. Our personal trainers will instruct & motivate you throughout your session, demonstrating exercises to ensure proper form and technique. Perfect for those who want instruction in the safety & comfort of your home.
